About Durbachati Youth Foundation

A community-based initiative formed on 01 May 2022 to uplift vulnerable children, youth and families.

Our Mission

To ensure that no child in Durbachati and nearby areas is deprived of education, basic healthcare or support in times of crisis.

Our Approach

Local volunteers identify genuine needs at village level, mobilise resources, and deliver direct help to families and students.

Key Programs

Practical, need-based support for students, patients and vulnerable families.

Education Support

  • Books, bags and stationery for school and college students.
  • Tuition help and guidance for examinations.

Emergency Medical Help

  • Support in arranging treatment and medicine for needy patients.
  • Connecting families with donors during medical emergencies.

Student & Youth Welfare

  • Counselling and motivation for students.
  • Youth volunteering and community service activities.
